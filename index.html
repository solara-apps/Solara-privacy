üì± How Your Data Flows

1. Voice Recording & Transcription

  • Recording: Captured on your device and securely transmitted for processing.
  • Processing: Audio files are processed exclusively on our private servers using OpenAI Whisper.
  • In-Memory Transcription: Generated transcripts are processed in RAM only, immediately sent back to your device, and never written to server storage.
  • Authentication: Protected by Supertokens tokens ‚Äì only authenticated users can access transcription.
  • Immediate Deletion (Audio): Audio files are automatically deleted from our servers within seconds after transcription.
  • Local Storage (Transcripts): Transcripts are stored securely on your device.
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses end-to-end encryption.

2. AI Enhancement & Summaries (Optional Premium Features)

  • Processing: When you choose AI features, only text transcripts are sent to Claude AI.
  • Privacy Protection: Requests include no personal identifiers ‚Äì only anonymous session IDs.
  • No Storage: We never store your journal content on our servers.
  • Secure Transit: All communications use enterprise-grade encryption.
  • Model Selection: Premium users get Sonnet, free users get Haiku.
  • Immediate Processing: AI responses are sent directly to your device and not retained.
