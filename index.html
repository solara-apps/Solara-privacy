Solara Voice Journal Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

Your privacy is fundamental to how we've designed Solara Voice Journal.
We believe your thoughts and reflections belong to you alone.

Our Privacy Promise

  • ✓ Your voice recordings never leave your device
  • ✓ We don't collect, store, or have access to your journal entries
  • ✓ All AI processing happens with privacy-first partners

How Your Data Flows

1. Voice Recording

  • Captured directly on your device
  • Stored locally in your app's private storage
  • Never uploaded to our servers

2. Transcription

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • No audio sent to external servers
  • Transcripts remain on your device

3. AI Enhancement (Optional)

  • When you choose to enhance entries, text is sent to Haiku
  • Haiku processes your text without storing it
  • Enhanced versions return to your device only

4. AI Summaries (Premium)

  • Summary generation uses Sonnet's advanced capabilities
  • Your entries are processed without permanent storage
  • Summaries are yours alone, stored locally

What We Don't Collect

  • ❌ Voice recordings
  • ❌ Journal entries
  • ❌ Personal reflections
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Usage analytics (unless you opt-in)

What We May Collect

  • Basic app functionality data (crashes, errors)
  • Subscription status (through App Store)
  • Anonymous usage statistics (only with your permission)

Third-Party Services

Haiku & Sonnet AI Services

  • Industry-leading privacy standards
  • No training on user data
  • Immediate deletion after processing
  • SOC 2 Type II certified

App Store

  • Subscription management
  • Required for app distribution
  • Governed by Apple's privacy policies

RevenueCat

  • Subscription processing and management
  • No access to your journal content
  • Privacy-focused analytics

Your Rights

  • Export all your data anytime
  • Delete everything with one tap
  • Full control over AI features
  • Complete ownership of your content

Data Storage

All journal data is stored exclusively on your device using industry-standard encryption. If you delete the app, all your data is permanently removed.

Security

Your journal entries are protected by:

  • iOS device encryption
  • App sandboxing
  • No cloud backups unless you enable iCloud (your choice)

Children's Privacy

Solara Voice Journal is designed for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of any material changes through the app. Continued use constitutes acceptance of updates.
