Solara Voice Journal Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

Your privacy is fundamental to how we've designed Solara Voice Journal.

We believe your thoughts and reflections belong to you alone.

Our Privacy Promise

  • Your voice recordings never leave your device
  • We don't collect, store, or have access to your journal entries
  • All AI processing happens with privacy-first partners

How Your Data Flows

1. Voice Recording

  • Captured directly on your device
  • Stored locally in your app's private storage
  • Never uploaded to our servers

2. Transcription

  • Processed entirely on your device (when available)
  • For cloud transcription: audio is sent securely to our AI partner
  • No audio sent to external servers is retained after processing
  • Transcriptions return to your device and are stored locally

3. AI Summaries

  • Generated from your transcriptions
  • Processed through secure AI services
  • No personal identifiers are included
  • Results stored only on your device

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal information
  • Voice recordings
  • Journal content
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics

Third-Party Services

AI Processing: We use enterprise-grade AI services through a secure self-hosted relay for transcription and summarization. These services process your data securely and don't retain it.

Subscription Management: RevenueCat handles premium subscriptions and processes payment information. They do not have access to your journal content.

Your Rights

  • Export: Export all your entries anytime
  • Delete: Remove any or all entries instantly
  • Control: Choose when to use AI features

Data Security

  • All data encrypted on your device
  • Secure connections for AI processing
  • No cloud backup of your personal content

Data Retention

Your recordings and journal entries remain on your device until you choose to delete them. We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve it if you delete the app.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We'll notify you of any significant changes through the app.
