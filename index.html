Solara Voice Journal Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

Your privacy is fundamental to how we've designed Solara Voice Journal.

We believe your thoughts and reflections belong to you alone.

Our Privacy Promise

  • Your voice recordings are stored only on your device
  • Transcription happens entirely on-device using Whisper Tiny
  • We don't collect, store, or have access to your journal entries
  • AI summaries are optional and processed with privacy in mind

How Your Data Flows

1. Voice Recording

  • Captured directly on your device
  • Stored locally in your app's private storage
  • Never uploaded to our servers

2. Transcription

  • Processed entirely on your device using OpenAI's Whisper Tiny model
  • No audio data ever leaves your device for transcription
  • Transcriptions are stored locally on your device
  • Works completely offline

3. AI Summaries & Insights (Optional Premium Features)

  • When you choose to summarize an entry, only the text transcript is sent to our AI service
  • We use Claude AI models (Sonnet for premium users, Haiku for free users)
  • Processed through our secure API relay - we never store your content
  • No personal identifiers are included with requests
  • AI-generated summaries return to your device and are stored locally

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal information
  • Voice recordings
  • Journal content (unless you explicitly use AI features)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers

Third-Party Services

AI Processing (Optional): When you use summary or insights features, we send your transcribed text to Anthropic's Claude AI (Sonnet or Haiku models) through our secure relay. These requests are:

  • Sent over encrypted connections
  • Not stored or logged by us
  • Not associated with any personal identifiers
  • Processed according to Anthropic's enterprise privacy standards

Subscription Management: RevenueCat handles premium subscriptions and processes payment information. They do not have access to your journal content.

Your Rights

  • Full Control: All processing is initiated by you
  • Export: Export all your entries anytime as text files
  • Delete: Remove any or all entries instantly
  • Offline Use: Core features work without internet

Data Security

  • All data stored in iOS's secure container on your device
  • Audio files and transcripts encrypted by iOS
  • Optional AI features use HTTPS encryption
  • No cloud backup or sync of your personal content

Data Retention

Your recordings and journal entries remain on your device until you choose to delete them. We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve it if you delete the app. When you use AI features, the text is processed immediately and not retained by our systems.

Children's Privacy

Solara Voice Journal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We'll notify you of any significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
